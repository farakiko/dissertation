2.2 JetNet

We introduce the JetNet dataset as a benchmark dataset for studies of fast simulation techniques in high-energy physics. It is derived from Ref. [? ],1 and comprises simulated particle jets with transverse momenta p

T ^jet≈1TeV ,originatingfromgluons,lightquarks,topquarks,andWandZbosonsproducedin13TeV proton − protoncollisionsthroughasimplifieddetector(Figure 2.1).

As disucssed in Chapter ??, each jets is represented as a point cloud, or particle cloud, of particles, with each particle represented as a node in the cloud with the three kinematic (p

T ^rel, η

rel,ϕ

rel)features.p

T,η,ϕ are the transverse momentum, pseudorapidity, and azimuthal angle, respectively, commonly used in collider physics and defined in Chapter ??. p

T ^relisthetransversemomentumoftheparticlerelativetothejetp

T, and η

Details of the simulations are as follows. The parton-level events are first produced at leading-order using MadGraph5_aMC@NLO 2.3.1 [? ] with the NNPDF 2.3LO1 parton distribution functions [? ]. To focus on a relatively narrow kinematic range, the transverse momenta of the partons and undecayed gauge bosons are generated in a window with energy spread given by Δp

T/ p

T = 0.01, centered at 1TeV. These parton-level events are then decayed and showered in pythia 8.212 [? ] with the Monash 2013 tune [? ], including the contribution from the underlying event. For each original particle type, 200,000 events are generated. Jets are clustered using the anti-kT algorithm [? ], with a distance parameter of R = 0.8 using the FastJet 3.1.3 and FastJet contrib 1.027 packages [? ? ]. Even though the parton-level p

T distributionisnarrow,thejetp

T spectrum is significantly broadened by kinematic recoil from the parton shower and energy migration in and out of the jet cone. We apply a restriction on the measured jet p

T < 1.6TeV for the p

T = 1TeV bin.ThisgenerationisasignificantlysimplifiedversionoftheofficialsimulationandreconstructionstepsusedforrealdetectorsattheLHC,toremainexperiment−independentaswellasallowpublicaccesstothedataset.
